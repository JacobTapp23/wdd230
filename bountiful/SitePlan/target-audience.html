Target Audience

Health-conscious individuals: People who prioritize nutrition and want to enjoy beverages made with fresh, high-quality ingredients. Busy professionals: Individuals who are on-the-go and need a quick and convenient way to order their favorite drinks without sacrificing quality. Fitness enthusiasts: People who are looking for drinks that can help them refuel after a workout or provide a boost of energy before exercise. Students: Individuals who are looking for a tasty and energizing drink to help them stay focused and alert during study sessions. Foodies: People who enjoy trying new and unique food and drink experiences and are willing to pay for high-quality, artisanal beverages.

Scenarios

1. Morning routine: A busy professional who wants to start their day with a nutritious and delicious breakfast smoothie can use the website to order a customized smoothie with their favorite ingredients and pick it up on the way to work.

2. Study break: A college student who needs a break from studying can order a specialty drink like a matcha latte or fruit smoothie to refresh and energize themselves during a study session.

3. Fitness recovery: A fitness enthusiast who just finished a workout can use the website to order a protein smoothie or other specialty drink to help refuel and recover their muscles after exercise.

4. Virtual meeting: A remote worker who is on a virtual meeting can use the website to order a specialty drink like a latte or tea to enjoy during the call, without having to leave their desk or office.

5. Special event: Someone planning a special event, like a wedding or corporate meeting, can use the website to place a large order of specialty drinks for guests, with customizable options to fit their preferences and dietary needs.
