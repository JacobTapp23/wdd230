Target Audience

The target audience for the chamber of commerce website will be residents and business owners of the Saupark area. The website will also provide services to tourists in the area looking for events

Personas

James is a small business owner in Saupark. He is 40 years old, and has been working in Saupark for 15 years. James is seeking ways to grow his busniess and expand his customer base through the use of social media.

Lauren is a new mom who just moved to Saupark when her husband accepted a job here. She is 26 years old and enjoys spending time with her daughter at the park. She also loves art and history.

Scenarios

1. Dave is seeking ways to grow his business, so he goes to the Saupark Chamber of Commerce website to join. This will give him the needed training and resources

2. Lauren gets on the site to see local musical events, museums, and art exhibits. She also looks for directions to the local park
